FAQ - TransitGo        

Frequently Asked Questions

 
   
     
How can I track my bus in real-time?
     
You can track your bus through the TransitGo mobile app or website by entering your route number or bus stop.
   
   
     
Is TransitGo free to use?
     
Yes, basic tracking services are completely free. Premium features may require a subscription.
   
   
     
Can I plan my journey in advance?
     
Absolutely! TransitGo allows you to plan your trip with estimated arrival and departure times.
   
   
     
Does TransitGo support multiple cities?
     
Currently, we operate in major cities across India, with plans to expand internationally soon.
   
   
     
How do I report an issue with a bus?
     
Use the 'Report Issue' feature in the app to notify us about delays, route errors, or other concerns.
   
   
     
Is my data safe with TransitGo?
     
Yes, we prioritize your privacy and use encrypted connections to protect your personal information.
   
   
     
Can I receive notifications for my bus?
     
Yes, enable push notifications on your device to receive real-time alerts for bus arrivals and delays.
   
   
     
Do you offer an offline mode?
     
Limited offline information like saved routes and schedules is available even without an internet connection.
   
   
     
How do I contact customer support?
     
You can contact us via email at support@transitgo.com or use the in-app support chat feature.
   
   
     
Is TransitGo eco-friendly?
     
Yes, by promoting public transportation usage, we help reduce carbon emissions and traffic congestion.
   
 
   
   
     
               

TransitGo makes your bus journey smarter, safer, and easier.
